<h3>Passwords - A Cracking Christmas (Day 9)</h3>

Day 9 focuses on password-based encryption and how to exploit locked files through dictionary attacks and mask attacks. The room challenges participants to crack the passwords of two locked files, and teaches how to use Linux command-line tools such as john and pdfcrack through hands-on exercises.

<h4>Concepts Explored:</h4>

- Password-based Encryption
- Dictionary Attacks
- Mask Attacks

<h4>Tools Used:</h4>

- Ubuntu Virtual Machine
- Pdfcrack
- John
- Zip2john
